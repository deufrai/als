  • Simple and Fast Tool ALS is designed to be easy to use and focuses on smooth performance, so you can concentrate on what truly matters: your images!
  • Keep Your Habits ALS adapts to your workflow. It automatically monitors capture folders and supports most formats, including RAW and FITS files.
  • Pre-Processing ALS automatically removes hot pixels and uses your dark masters for sharper images.
  • Alignment and Stacking ALS automatically aligns and stacks each image to achieve optimal quality.
  • Free Navigation Freely explore the stacked results. Zoom and navigate to discover every detail.
  • Image Controls Adjust levels and color balance to your liking with ALS controls.
  • Easy Sharing Easily share your images via ALS's built-in web server.
  • Various Output Formats Save your images in various formats for maximum flexibility.
